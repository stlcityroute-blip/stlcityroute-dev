Advertising Policy

Who can advertise

Businesses operating in the City of St. Louis or the greater metropolitan area whose services are directly relevant to city field work. All listings are reviewed before publication.

What's included

Business name, phone number, website link, and a short description. Standard and Featured listings are limited to one line of description. Exclusive listings may include two lines.

Exclusivity

Only one Exclusive sponsor per category. Featured slots are first-come, first-served — one per category. Standard listings have no slot limit. Exclusive sponsors hold their category as long as the listing remains active and renewed.

Deployment

Listings go live within 48 hours of confirmed payment. Updates to listing information are included at no extra charge.

Payment and renewal

Annual billing. Invoice or Venmo accepted. Renewal reminder sent 30 days before expiration. Lapsed listings are removed and the slot re-opens.

Content standards

Listings must accurately describe the business and its services. No false claims, no misleading descriptions. STLCityRoute reserves the right to reject or remove any listing that does not meet professional standards or is not relevant to city field work.

No editorial influence

Advertising does not influence app features, city contacts, or any other app content. Paid listings are clearly placed in the "Local Business Listings" section and never mixed with official city resources.
